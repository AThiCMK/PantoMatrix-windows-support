Adventages of BEAT

  • 2-type recording

    Each speaker read predefined text (50%) and chat with director (50%).

  • 3-Class Semantic

    Annotatied detectic, iconic, metaphoic gestures in 3 intensity levels.

  • 4-Modality Data

    120FPS mocap body gestures; 60FPS&52D (iphone ARkit) facial expression blendshape weights; 16KHZ audio; proofread text scripts.

  • 4-language

    Speakers are talking with English-Native (34h), English-Fluent (26h), Chinese (12h), Spinish (2h) and Japanese (2h).

  • 8-Class Emotion

    Speakers will read the text in Neutral (51%), and Emotional (7%x7), i.e., Happiness, Anger, Sadness, Contempt, Surprise, Fear, Disgust.
